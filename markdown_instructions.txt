#title - highlighting the titles. The number of characters "#" sets the header level
= or - : this underscore (at least 3 in a row) highlights the headings of the first (=) or second (-) level
**bold font** or __bold font__: this type of selection is used for bold font
*italic font* or _cursive font_: this type of selection is used for bold font
***bold italics***: this type of selection is used for bold italics
~~strikethrough text~~: this type of selection is used for strikethrough
*at the beginning of the line - unnumbered lists
1, 2, 3 at the beginning of the line - numbered lists
